# Keyword Research

Research keywords for a given topic, niche, or seed keyword.

## Steps

1. Take the seed keyword/topic and target region
2. Use SE Ranking keyword tools to pull:
   - Similar keywords
   - Related keywords
   - Long-tail keywords
   - Keyword questions (for FAQ/content ideas)
3. For each keyword gather: search volume, keyword difficulty, CPC, competition
4. Cluster keywords by intent:
   - **Informational**: how, what, why, guide, tutorial
   - **Commercial**: best, top, review, comparison, vs
   - **Transactional**: buy, price, order, discount, near me
   - **Navigational**: brand-specific queries
5. Identify quick wins: high volume + low difficulty

## Output format

Group keywords by cluster. For each cluster show a table:

| Keyword | Volume | KD | CPC | Intent |
|---------|--------|----|-----|--------|

End with a "Recommended Priority" section — top 10 keywords to target first.
